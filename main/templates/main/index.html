Welcome to Leftover Ingredients!

Tired of having leftover ingredients inside your house and don't know what to do with it? Guilty of throwing it out in the trash? Hungry right now as well? Yeah I can tell. Well you came to the perfect spot. All of your problems will be solved now!

Mushroom and Brussels Sprouts Pizza

A ball of store-bought dough is the easiest way to experience freshly made pizza without calling delivery. This pie layers in fontina cheese and Shiitake mushrooms amid bright balsamic glaze and crunchy sprouts. It's a rustic 25-minute dinner you'll simply love.

Veggie Medley on Sweet Potatoes

A delicious fall-time vegetarian dinner, this bright bowl of sweet spuds is blended into a silky bed for fresh veggies thanks to the addition of Greek yogurt and (plenty of) butter. It's all ready in just under 25 mins!
